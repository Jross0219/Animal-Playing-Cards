Giraffe

A giraffe has four stomachs, three hearts, and three brains. Giraffe are extremly intelligent when it coomes time to eat...

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa
  • Average Length: Males up to 18 feet; Females up to 14 feet
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 26 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands and open woodlands

Giraffes are the worlds tallest mammals, Ranging from 14-18 feet. A baby giraffe is called a calf. Depending on where they live and what they eat, their color can range from light tan to almost black. Giraffes are mostly in East Africa in the grassland and open woodlands.
